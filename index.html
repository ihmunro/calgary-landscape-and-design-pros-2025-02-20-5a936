About Calgary Landscape

Founded in 2008, Calgary Landscape and Design Pros emerged from a passion for creating exceptional outdoor spaces that enhance the beauty of our city. What started as a small team of dedicated landscapers has grown into Calgary's most trusted full-service landscaping company, with over 1,000 successfully completed projects across the region.

Our mission is to transform outdoor spaces into sustainable, beautiful environments that bring joy to our clients while respecting our local ecosystem. We're committed to using eco-friendly practices, supporting local suppliers, and maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship in every project we undertake. Our core values of integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do.
